# Trusted Home Services Platform - Simple Requirements

## Problem
In Pakistan, it is hard to find reliable plumbers, electricians, AC repair technicians, and maids.  
People don’t trust unknown workers, and service quality is not consistent.

## Solution
A platform where users can book **verified workers** with confidence.

## Main Features
- **Verified Workers** (ID check, background check)  
- **User Reviews & Ratings** (feedback from real customers)  
- **Easy Online Booking** (choose service, set time, confirm)  
- **Emergency Service** (worker arrives in 30–60 minutes)

## Extra Features
- Online or cash payments  
- Service guarantee (e.g., 7-day warranty)  
- Rewards for regular users  
- Urdu + English support  

## Why People Need This
- Trust issues with random workers  
- Saves time and effort  
- Safer and more reliable  
- Fits busy city lifestyle  

## Target Users
- Families in big cities (Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad)  
- Working professionals who need quick help  
- Anyone who values safety and trust  

## Success Goals
- Many verified workers onboarded  
- High average ratings (4.5+ stars)  
- Fast emergency response (<45 minutes)  
- More repeat bookings  

## Future Ideas
- Subscription for regular home maintenance  
- Corporate services (office cleaning, repairs)  
- Smart home integration in the future
